Professor of Geostrategic Studies | University of Haifa

Researching terrorism, political extremism, special operations, and the changing character of warfare.

Prof. Ami Pedahzur teaches in the Department of Middle Eastern and Islamic Studies at the University of Haifa and directs the Chaikin Institute for Geostrategy. His work examines terrorism, political violence, special operations forces, modern warfare, and the methods used to study security and conflict.
