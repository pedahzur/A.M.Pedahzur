About

Prof. Ami Pedahzur is a Professor of Geostrategic Studies in the Department of Middle Eastern and Islamic Studies at the University of Haifa and the Director of the Chaikin Institute for Geostrategy. His research spans terrorism and political violence, special operations forces, modern warfare, political extremism, and research methods in security studies.

His work has been published in leading journals and by major academic publishers including Oxford University Press, Columbia University Press, Cambridge University Press, and Routledge.
