Professor of Geostrategic Studies, University of Haifa

Prof. Ami Pedahzur

I am a Professor of Geostrategic Studies in the Department of Middle Eastern and Islamic Studies at the University of Haifa and Director of the Chaikin Institute for Geostrategy. My research focuses on modern warfare, terrorism, political extremism, special operations forces, and research methods.

Research Areas

Special Operations Forces

The global rise of Israeli special operations forces, their doctrine, structure, and international influence on counterterrorism and military strategy.

Terrorism Studies

Terrorism, counterterrorism, suicide attacks, and political violence in comparative perspective.

Modern Warfare

Technology-human interaction in contemporary conflict, human-machine teaming, and military adaptation.

Political Extremism

The Israeli radical right, political violence, and democratic resilience.

Research Methods

AI-enhanced research methodologies, social network analysis, and quantitative approaches to security studies.

Geostrategy

Middle Eastern security, Israeli national security policy, and civil-military relations.
